About the U.S. Citizenship Test

This website is designed to help you prepare for the U.S. Citizenship Test. The test consists of 128 civics questions. During the naturalization interview, applicants will be asked up to 20 civics questions from the list of 128 questions. An applicant must answer correctly at least 12 of the 20 questions to pass the civics test.

About This Website

This website provides a comprehensive way to study for the test. You can view all questions, filter them by category and subcategory, and practice with flashcards.

Source

The content on this website is based on the official USCIS materials: 2025 Civics Test - 128 Questions and Answers.
